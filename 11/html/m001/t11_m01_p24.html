Step 24

  • Align the matchmark on the forward spinner with the matchmark on the aft spinner and install the forward spinner.
  • Before securing the nuts against the spinner, the forward spinner must be rotated clockwise against the slot end on the forward spinner stud holes. This retains the nuts in position on the forward spinner.
  • Secure the forward spinner with eight self-locking nuts and torque per manual value.
