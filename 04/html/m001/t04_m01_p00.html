Identification

The engine Indicating System consists of the following:

  • Engine sensors
  • EICAS which is on the primary display in the flight compartment.
  • The engine parameters displayed are:
    • N1 - Fan rotor speed
    • Inter turbine temperature ITT
    • N2 - Compressor rotor speed
    • Fuel flow
    • Oil temperature
    • Oil pressure
    • Fan and Core vibration
