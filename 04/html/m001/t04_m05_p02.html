Operation
  • The signals generated by the vibration transducers (one per engine) are carried by shielded cables to the signal conditioner in the avionics bay.
  • The signal conditioner processes the signals and provides an output for display on EICAS.
  • The fan, or N1, indication is a continuous sweep hand indication.
  • The core, or N2, indication is displayed only when the vibration level has exceeded a max limit.
    • Vibration indication is shown within the N2 speed indication dial.
