Maintenance Practices

  • Since the segments have different total probe immersions, they will sense different temperature patterns.
  • The engine temperature pattern dictates if the left or right thermocouple segment will show a higher ITT.
    • In most engines, the left segment will show higher ITT.
  • If ITT shows lower than usual, examine the right segment first.
  • If ITT shows higher than usual, examine the left segment first.
