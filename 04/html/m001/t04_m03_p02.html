Operation
  • Within the Alternator are two windings
    • One provides the N2 signal to the EICAS.
    • One provides power to the N1 Amplifier located in the aft equipment bay.
  • The alternator:
    • Splined to the gearbox.
    • Outputs a frequency proportional to the N2 rotor which drives the gearbox.
