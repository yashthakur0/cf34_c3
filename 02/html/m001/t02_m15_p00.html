Identification

The core cowl assembly

  • Located aft of the forward core cowl assembly, around the center section of the engine
  • Consists of the upper cowl door and the lower cowl door.
    • Five adjustable quick-release latches, installed on the lower cowl door, hold the doors in a closed position.
    • The inside of each door is provided with a strut.
  • The upper cowl door has a blow-out panel held by a spring loaded cam.
  • The lower cowl door has a drain mast at the bottom.
