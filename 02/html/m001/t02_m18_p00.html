Identification

The exhaust nozzle assembly

  • Rearmost section of the core cowling, located aft of the core cowl assembly.
  • Consists of the following:
    • Exhaust nozzle
    • Exhaust nozzle fairing
  • Made from a special reinforced material.
  • Attached to the rear flange of the engine.
  • The exhaust nozzle fairing is a strong, monocoque structure made from aluminum with a corrosion resistant steel heat shield attached to the inside.
  • Three brackets in the exhaust nozzle fairing attach to three support assemblies which are installed on the rear flange of the engine.
  • The fairing also has:
    • A double loop fire-detection system
    • Two overboard drain fittings
    • Three access panels
