Purpose

The purpose of the engine cowling is as follows:

    • To provide a smooth surface for airflow with no restriction around the engine.
    • To provide fire containment by isolating the engine from the airframe systems in case of engine fire.
    • To provide protection to the engine components and assemblies.
    • To help cool the engine core.
    • To provide access to the engine LRUs.
    • To be part of the thrust reverser system.
