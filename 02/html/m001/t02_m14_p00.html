Identification

The forward core cowl assembly

  • Forms the front section of the core cowling.
  • Located aft of the fan cowling section and is enclosed within it.
  • Divided into three interchangeable modules.
    • Upper module
    • Lower module
    • Fixed core cowl module
  • The three modules include the thrust reverser blocker-door linkage assemblies and core cowl panels attached to two mount rings.
    • The forward mount ring attaches to the rear of the engine fan case.
    • The rear mount ring holds the forward edges of the core cowl assembly doors.
    • The forward core cowl assembly is attached on one side to the fixed core cowl.
