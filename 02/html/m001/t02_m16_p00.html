Identification

The fixed core cowl:

  • Longitudinal section that is located on the airframe-facing side of the engine.
  • The front of the fixed core cowl is attached to the forward core cowl assembly.
  • The rear of the fixed core cowl is attached to the engine by three turnbuckle links.
  • The side of the fixed core cowl is connected to the service pylon.
  • The fixed core cowl has openings for the passage of ducts, hoses, electrical harnesses, throttle controls and the engine thrust mount.
