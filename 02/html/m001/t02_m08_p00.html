Identification

The nose inlet cowl

  • Located at the front of the fan cowling.
  • Comprises of the forward and aft bulkheads and a circular engine attachment ring.
  • The forward bulkhead is formed by:
    • The outer barrel
    • The inner barrel
    • The lipskin
    • Forms a smooth, cylindrical opening called the leading edge.
    • Contains the ’D’ duct for lipskin anti-icing.
  • The aft bulkhead supports the outer barrel and the inner barrel.
  • The engine attachment ring fastens the aft portion of the nose inlet cowl to the front of the fan containment case.
  • The inlet area of the nose inlet cowl is lined with sound attenuation material.
