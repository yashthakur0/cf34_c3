Purpose

The core cowl assembly serves the following purpose:

    • It protects the engine from external damage.
    • The cowl doors provide access to the engine.
    • It provides cooling airflow for the engine.
    • The upper cowl door contains a blow-out panel that is designed to blow out and open the door when the pressure inside becomes too high.
    • It allows the gang drain mast to exit to the atmosphere.
