Identification

  • When transporting the engine, particular care needs to be taken in the following areas:
    • Removal from aircraft
    • Loading and unloading
    • Storage and preservation
    • Transportation
  • When removing the engine from the aircraft:
    • carefully disconnect the external power
    • remove the access panels, cowlings and pylon interface connections.
  • For loading and unloading the engine, always use the recommended hoist with the engine hoist adapter.
  • When unloading the engine:
    • It should be aligned over the horizontal assembly dolly 2C91064.
    • It should be carefully lowered on the dolly and fastened using the forward engine supports and the quick-release pins.
  • The engine can be moved over short distances (within the engine shop) on the horizontal assembly dolly 2C91064.
  • For longer distances, use an air-ride trailer fitted with spring-loaded/pneumatic suspensions and wheels made from shock-absorbing materials.
