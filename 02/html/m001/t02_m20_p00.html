Identification
  • At the forward end, the engine is supported by two forward mounts located on the aft fan case.
    • The forward mounts are attached to the engine mount frame on the airframe pylon by a yoke and two spherical mount bearings.
  • At the aft end, the engine is supported by a thrust mount assembly.
  • The thrust mount assembly
    • Mounted to the combustion chamber frame by an expandable bolt and to the rear mount ring by a shear bolt.
    • Attached to the airframe pylon by a thrust fitting, a thrust shaft, a tapered spacer and a pylon bushing.
  • Each engine is provided with four forward mount lugs for installing it on either the left side or the right side of the airframe.
    • Each engine is shipped with only one thrustmount assembly.
    • Attachment points exist on both sides of the engine to position the thrust mount assembly for either left or right engine installation.
