Purpose

The torque box serves the following purpose:

    • It provides structural support to various thrust reverser components.
    • When the thrust reverser is deployed, the torque box moves rearward, thus transmitting the motion of the blocker doors to the pylon tracks.
    • The backward movement of the torque box provides space for the blocked airflow to escape.
    • The sound attenuation panels in the torque box help to dampen the noise of the airflow when the thrust reverser is deployed.
