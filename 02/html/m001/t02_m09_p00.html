Identification

The upper and lower access cowls:

  • Located aft of the nose inlet cowl.

    • The front portion of the two access cowls is attached to a flange on the outer barrel of the fan inlet cowl.
    • The side portion of the two access cowls is attached to the fixed nose cowl.
  • Made of special composite materials.
    • The outer surface of the cowls is covered with a layer of aluminized fabric ply.
  • Held in a closed position by quick-release fasteners.
  • The lower access cowl has a panel that can be opened.
