Purpose

The nose inlet cowl:

  • Provides the engine fan with unrestricted and smooth airflow.
  • Directs the remaining airflow over the engine nacelle.
  • Contains the anti-ice assembly which prevents ice formation around the leading edge.
  • Contains the installation brackets for the thrust reverser actuators.
  • Contains sound attenuation material in the nose inlet cowl helps to dampen the noise of air suction.
