Purpose

The flightline/ground transportation requirements:

  • The aircraft does not suffer damage during removal of the engine.
  • The engine does not suffer damage during the loading and unloading operation.
  • Personnel do not suffer injuries when loading and unloading the engine.
  • The engine and its components do not suffer damage during prolonged storage.
  • The engine is transported safely without suffering shocks and vibrations.
