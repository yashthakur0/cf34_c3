Purpose

The exhaust nozzle assembly serves the following purpose:

    • It directs the engine exhaust gases overboard.
    • The exhaust nozzle fairing provides a smooth, aerodynamic surface for external airflow and controls the flow of engine bypass air to keep the exhaust nozzle temperature to satisfactory levels.
    • The fire-detection system provides warning signals to the cockpit in the event of an engine fire.
    • The two drain fittings provide outlet for the drainage of fluids and vapors from the combustor drain and the C-sump drain.
