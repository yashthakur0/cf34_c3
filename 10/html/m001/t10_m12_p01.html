Operation

Located below the fuel pump, hydraulic pump and the starter pads are leakage detection ports. These are designed to collect any leakage from the carbon seal of the component above it's position. They will fill with oil and help in indicating to maintenance personnel which carbon seal is leaking. An indication will also be evident at the 6 o'clock gang drain tube as all AGB pads are connected together internally and lead down to the drain.
