Maintenance Practices

  • If significant oil is accumulating inside the center vent tube, remove the #7 carbon seal assembly and replace.
  • If wetness is noted on the outside of the exhaust centerbody around the vent opening, dry off and run the engine.
  • If wetness reappears and is combined with higher than normal oil accumulation inside the center vent tube, replace the carbon seal assembly.
