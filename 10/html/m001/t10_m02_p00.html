Identification

  • There are two types of engine faults:
    • Faults that are included in the aircraft Minimum Equipment List (MEL).
    • Faults that are NOT included in the aircraft Minimum Equipment List (MEL).
  • Faults included in the MEL do not require immediate attention before a flight, and the aircraft can be allowed to take off.
    • These faults should be rectified after the flight or during the next maintenance check.
  • Faults NOT included in the MEL require immediate attention.
    • Engine should be shutdown and maintenance performed to correct the faults.
