Identification

Engine vibration is monitored in the cockpit on EICAS in order to detect any engine problems that would cause a high vibration on either the N1 (Fan) or N2 (Core) rotors. The causes for high vibration can be identified for each rotor. The signal vibration pickup/accelerometer is located on the front frame/compressor forward flange at the 2 o'clock position.
