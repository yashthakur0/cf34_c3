Maintenance Practices

  • If a #3 carbon seal leak is verified, remove and replace the carbon seal assembly.
  • If wetness is seen on the outside of the compressor casing and there is a question as to whether it is coming from inside the case from an internal #3 seal leak, check the airframe bleed ducting and coalescer socks. Both should be clean if there is no internal leak.
  • If there is further findings that would indicate a #3 seal leak, and there is some doubt as to whether an internal leak exists, it is recommended that the outside of the engine be cleaned and a compressor water wash be accomplished. Continue operation and monitor for further indications.
