Functional Description

Leakage from the #7 carbon seal will be evident as follows:

  • Increased oil consumption
  • Significant oil puddling inside the C-sump cover vent tube aft of the carbon seal. Note that some degree of wetness inside the center vent tube can be expected; oil accumulation of about a teaspoon is normal since the #7 carbon seal is not pressurized.
  • Wetness on the outside of the exhaust centerbody around the vent opening.
