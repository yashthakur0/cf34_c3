Operation
  • The sensor is mounted such that the No.2 bearing locknut rotates under the sensor.
  • The locknut has 28 teeth or castellation and the rotation of the nut intermittently bends the magnetic field set up by the permanent magnet and induces a pulsating voltage in the coil.
  • The signal is transmitted to the control amplifier to provide a signal of fan speed.
