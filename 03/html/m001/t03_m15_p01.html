Purpose

The N1 control amplifier has the following functions:

  1. Control fan speed as a function of PLA and T2, when N2 is above 79.1%, by controlling the fuel flow across the metering valve through the MFC torquemotor.
  2. Provides the torquemotor with an error signal to increase N1 by 169 rpm in the event of an engine failure during take off.
  3. Provides N1, N2, and auxiliary power reserve (APR) indications to the cockpit.
