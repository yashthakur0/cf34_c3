Operation
  • The two windings within the alternator are energized from rotation of the AGB.
    • One winding supplies electrical power to the N1 control amplifier.
    • The second winding provides a frequency signal to the cockpit that is an indication of compressor rotor speed (N2).
  • The N2 signal is sent through the N1 control amplifier as signal path to the cockpit.
