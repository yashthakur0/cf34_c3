Identification
  • The N2 governor is a hydromechanical speed governing device which controls N2 as a function of PLA input.
  • Fuel for combustion must pass through the metering valve and the factors controlling it's position is critical to engine operation.
  • The governing system is part of the MFC and is made up of:
    • N2 throttle cam
    • Cam follower
    • Speeder spring
    • Flyweights
    • N2 governor servo
