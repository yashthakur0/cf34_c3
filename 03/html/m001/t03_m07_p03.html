Maintenance practices

  • At high altitude airports above 4600 feet, the idle speed cannot be directly adjusted through the idle adjustment screw, because the idle reset cam is in contact with the idle speed lever.
    • In such cases, a special tool is used to apply pressure the P0 sensor fitting to make the idle reset cam rotate away from the idle speed lever.
    • This brings the fuel control down below 4600 feet. For additional information, refer to the testing section in the Engine Manuel.
