Purpose
  • The purpose of the N1 control system is to control the fuel flow to the engine at higher power conditions above 79.1% N2.
  • The fan produces approximately 85% of the thrust of the engine and is the best indicator of engine power.
    • The greater the N1 speed, the greater the thrust output.
  • Different engines produce varying amounts of thrust for the same N2, thus the relationship between N2 and N1 can vary from engine to engine.
  • The N1 control system establishes direct control over N1 and makes sure at high power settings, N1 is varied as a function of PLA and T2.
    • The result is that the two engines operating at high power settings, will have the same N1 and thrust at a given PLA and T2.
