Purpose
  • The engine inputs ensure proper operation of the N1 control system.
  • The power for the N1 amplifier is supplied from the engine driven alternator mounted on the AGB.
  • The reference curves that are factory installed, are dependent on the inputs of PLA from the MFC and T2 from the fan inlet.
  • The N1 sensor inputs the actual N1.
  • The rate signal from the MFC is used to ensure the engine will not overshoot on acceleration.
