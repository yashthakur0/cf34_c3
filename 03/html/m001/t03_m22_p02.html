Operation
  • During an acceleration or deceleration, the PLA on the MFC also increases or decreases correspondingly which causes the N1 throttle cam to rotate.
  • Depending on the angle of the N1 throttle cam, a corresponding radius of the cam is in contact with the LVDT (PLA).
  • This translates the physical value of the cam radius into an electrical signal that is transmitted to the N1 control amplifier.
