Operation
  • A mechanical reference curve is integral to the MFC. For any given PLA, there is a corresponding N2 speed irregardless of any external factors.
  • In N2 control, the engine will reach maximum N2 at approximately 85 degrees PLA.
  • The only area on the reference curve not controlled by PLA is idle.
    • Idle is controlled by temperature, not PLA.
