Identification
  • The compressor rotor speed (N2) is provided to the MFC by the fuel pump through a coupling shaft.
    • One end of the coupling shaft is splined to the fuel pump and the other is splined to the MFC.
  • The rotational movement of the compressor rotor is transmitted to the fuel pump through the power take off assembly, radial drive shaft and the AGB.
    • This rotates the fuel pump and N2 is transmitted by the coupling shaft to the MFC.
