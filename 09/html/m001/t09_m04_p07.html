Operation

Venting of the air from the sumps and oil system is accomplished through a "centervent" system. Air, used to pressurize the sump seals in A and C sump vent directly into the centervent, or LPT shaft, and then to ambient. Air from B sump must first cross the B sump vent regulator valve into A sump then to centervent. Air, separated in the oil tank from the scavenged oil is sent across the oil tank pressurizing valve into A sump through the 12 o'clock strut, then to centervent.
