Operation

The replenishment system is operated from a location in the aft equipment bay of the aircraft. When the panel switch is set to "on" and the selector valve is manually positioned for the proper engine, an electrical signal is sent to the transfer pump, starting the oil flow. When the oil tank is full, the signal conditioner receives a signal from the oil level sensor within the tank and automatically shuts down the transfer pump.
