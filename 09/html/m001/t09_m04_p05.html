Identification

The engine vent sub-system collects air that has been used for seal pressurization in all sumps and vents it to atmosphere. Air is also separated from the scavenged oil and routed to the engine vent system. Vent system components include:

  • Low Pressure Turbine Shaft (LPT)
  • B-sump vent regulator valve
  • Air oil separator in the oil tank.
