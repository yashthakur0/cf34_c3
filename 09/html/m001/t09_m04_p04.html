Operation

Scavenge is accomplished through separate elements within the lube and scavenge pump. Separate forward and aft scavenge oil flow returns to the pump housing from B and C sump. Return oil from the A sump and AGB are internally feed through ports on the face of the pump housing. All scavenge oil is then returned to the oil tank, first passing across the master chip detector, then the air is separated in the oil tank by the use of a cyclone type deaerator.
