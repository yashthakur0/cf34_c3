Operation

The system modification included the removal of the A-sump scavenge pump from the gearbox which allowed the A-sump oil to return, by gravity drain, into the Accessory Gearbox and drawn back into the system by the AGB scavenge elements of the lube and scavenge pump. The B-sump vent regulator valve was also modified, removing the piston and spring from the valve and designing orifices into the valve fittings to permit the designed vent flow from B-sump into A-sump under all operating conditions. This ensures that the proper pressure is maintained within B-sump, setting the proper seal pressurization. A reference line, which is no longer required, was also removed from the valve to the LPT module.
