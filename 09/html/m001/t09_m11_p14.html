Operation

When the differential across the filter increases to a level of 21 - 26 psid, the sensor switch closes and provides a signal of impending bypass to the oil -detection panel. To eliminate erroneous oil filter impending bypass indications caused by cold oil temperatures resulting in high viscosity of the oil, the sensor incorporates a thermal lockout feature. The sensor is inactivated for oil temperatures below 100 -130 deg F or 38-54 deg C.
