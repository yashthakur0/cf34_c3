Maintenance practices

  • Maintenance tip #1. The scavenge screens from the B and C sumps have been removed, by service bulletin, from the fittings into the scavenge elements, but the AGB screens remain in position at the internal ported fittings of the pump face.
  • Maintenance tip #2. The anti-leak check valve could stick in the open position and allow the oil to drain through the pump into the gearbox over time. This gives an indication of low oil level when checked, but if serviced under this condition, will cause over servicing of the engine. Over servicing causes low oil pressure combined with high oil temperatures which could led to an IFSD. If the engine shows a low oil level when checked and was not serviced within the recommended time period, motor the engine for 30 seconds and then check the oil level.
