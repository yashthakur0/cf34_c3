Identification

The engine oil tank is located at the 10 o'clock position on the compressor case. The oil tank is an assembly, made up of the following components:

  • Tank body
  • Oil fill cap and adapter
  • Tank pressurizing valve
  • Tank vent air out boss
  • Airframe level sensing and remote fill port
  • Scavenge oil in boss
  • Self closing valve
  • Electrical master chip detector
  • Supply oil out boss
