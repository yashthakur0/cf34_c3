Identification

The main lube and scavenge pump is located on the aft face of the accessory gearbox at the 7 o'clock position. The components that make up the pump are:

  • Seven positive displacement gerotor elements which are mounted on a single shaft and driven by the AGB gearing
  • An oil filter, held in the pump by a filter housing threaded into a steel insert
  • A system relief valve
  • An oil filter bypass valve
  • An anti-leak check valve
