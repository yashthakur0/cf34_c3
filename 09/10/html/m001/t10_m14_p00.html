Identification

The fan vibration level is shown on the EICAS primary page. The indication will change from green to amber when a vibration level of 2.7 mil is reached. The cause of high fan vibration can result from:

  • Indication system problems
  • Icing conditions
  • Damaged fan blades
  • Fan blades that are not free to move at the retaining pin attach point
