Purpose

  • The Fault Isolation section (72-00-00 Fault Isolation) in the Engine Manual contains procedures for recognizing and troubleshooting common engine faults. Each procedure contains information such as:
    • General description of the fault
    • Tools, equipment and materials needed to repair the fault
    • List of symptoms and possible causes for the fault
    • Troubleshooting procedures for each symptom
    • Key questions to ask before troubleshooting a symptom
    • Fault Isolation figures showing the troubleshooting steps to be followed for each symptom
  • The Field Service Engineer (FSE) provides on-site technical support to the customer.
  • Product Support Engineering (PSE) provides engineering support to the Field Service Engineer (FSE) and the customer.
  • The Customer Support Center provides technical and engineering support directly to the customer. The Customer Support Center can be contacted 24 hours-a-day, 7 days-a-week by calling the following telephone numbers:
    • Toll Free: 1-877-GEAE-CSC (1-877-432-3329)
    • International: 513-552-3272
  • The Customer Web Center is a website for GEAE customers and contains information such as spare parts order status, technical manuals, etc.
