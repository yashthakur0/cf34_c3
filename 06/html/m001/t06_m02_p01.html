Purpose

  • The W2 cable carries the following:
    • Power supply and N2 signal from the alternator to the N1 control amplifier.
    • N1 signal from the fan speed sensor to the N1 control amplifier.
    • Torque motor signal from the N1 control amplifier to the main fuel control.
    • Metering valve rate feedback signal from the MFC to the N1 control amplifier.
  • Yellow cable carries the output from airframe connectors E3 and E7 to the N1 control amplifier connectors E5 and E8.
  • Ignition leads W6 and W7 carry ignition exciter output power to the ignitor plugs.
  • The W3 cable carries the ITT indication to the flight deck.
