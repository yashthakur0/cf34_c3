Purpose

Bearings 1, 2 and 3 are located in A-sump. Bearings No.1 and 3 are ball bearings which absorb both axial and radial loads, and are the thrust bearings for the N1 and N2 rotors. Bearing No. 2 is a roller bearing for absorbing radial loads.

In B-sump both bearings, No. 4 and 5 are roller type and support the high pressure turbine and high pressure compressor rotor.

C-sump bearings No. 6 and 7 also are both roller type and support the low pressure turbine rotor.
