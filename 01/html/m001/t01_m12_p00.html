Identification

The compressor module consists of the following:

  • Compressor front frame
  • Compressor rotor assembly
  • Compressor stator assembly
  • Power takeoff assembly

The compressor front frame is the first major structural support and houses the A-sump. The compressor rotor assembly is made up of 14 stages of compressor blades and the compressor stator case surrounds the rotor and consists of two steel casing halves split on the horizontal line. The power takeoff assembly is splined to the compressor forward shaft and is in the A-sump.
