Functional Description

The front frame provides the structural support to the A-sump. The struts are used to route the following servicing lines and sensors into and out of the sump:

  • Oil tank vent and B-sump vent
  • 7th stage regulated seal air pressure
  • Oil scavenge
  • Radial drive shaft
  • Seal drain
  • Oil supply
  • N1 speed sensor
