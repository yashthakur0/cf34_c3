Purpose

The accessory gearbox provides drive to the various engine and airframe accessories that are mounted on pads, or axis locations, on the forward and aft side of the AGB. They are:

  • Fuel pump
  • Lube pump
  • IDG
  • Hydraulic pump
  • Starter
  • Alternator
  • A-sump scavenge pump

The A-sump scavenge pump was removed from production in 1998, which then changed the flow of scavenge oil through a gravity drain line into the AGB and allowed for most scavenge oil to drain into the AGB through the six o'clock strut. The AGB is driven through the power takeoff assembly (PTO) which is splined to the compressor forward shaft in A-sump, and a radial drive shaft that is splined to the PTO and the AGB drive pad through the six o'clock strut.
