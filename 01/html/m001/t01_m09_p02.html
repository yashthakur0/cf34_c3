Maintenance practices

The fan blades, fan disk, retaining pins, forward shaft and the aft spinner constitute a balanced assembly. When performing the fan blade lubrication procedure or in changing the No. 1 carbon seal, all blades must be numbered to ensure they are placed back in the same location. If not, an imbalance will occur.

The components of the fan rotor assembly have an "0" balance mark applied at assembly so that they can be reinstalled in the same position, which will prevent an imbalance. The notch placed in the aft spinner indicates the alignment with the No.1 blade, which aligns with the "0" mark on the disk and on the forward shaft.

Fan blade lubrication is required for the CF34-3 engine. Over time, the lubrication will wear, resulting in an increase in fan vibration. Click on the attached DTA to review the procedure.
