Functional description

Air enters the transition assembly, passing through the stage 3 nozzle and into the stage 3 rotor blades. It continues through stages 4-6 nozzles imparting rotation to stages 4-6 rotor blades. The air then exits the engine through the exhaust frame. The exhaust frame is the third major structural support in the engine and houses the C-sump and is used to support the No. 6 and 7 bearings.
