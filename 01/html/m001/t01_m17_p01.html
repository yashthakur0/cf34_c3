Purpose

The turbine transition assembly provides the flowpath for exhaust gases from the high pressure turbine to the low pressure turbine. The outer and inner liner and seal comprise the flowpath for air through the assembly. The exhaust gas flows through the stage 3 nozzle, which directs and accelerates the air into the stage 3 rotor of the LPT. The transition casing provides a frame for the entire assembly. Bosses on the casing and through the outer transition liner allow for the installation of the T5.4 thermocouple harnesses, measuring the temperature of the gases entering the low pressure turbine area through a series of probes into the gas path.
