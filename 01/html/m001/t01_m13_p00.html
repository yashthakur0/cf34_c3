Identification

The compressor rotor for the CF34-3A1 is a 14 stage, axial flow compressor consisting of:

  • A stage 1 and a stage 2 disk with blades installed axially.
  • A stage 3 through 8 spool.
  • A stage 9 disk and a stage 10 through 14 stage spool.

The stage 3 through 14 blades have circumferential dovetails which are assembled through loading slots in the spools and stage 9 disk. The positive locking of the blades in each stage is done by the use of a half nut/ set screw blade retention system. A rear shaft is splined to the high pressure turbine.
