Purpose

The station designation system is used for easily identifying certain parameters along the axial line such as temperatures and pressures. The examples shown are established station designations representing locations or sensors. Some of the sensors locations are used in the control system.

The T2, or fan inlet temperature, is an input to the N1 amplifier, located in the aft equipment bay. The T2C, or compressor inlet temperature sensor, is an input to the main fuel control and is located in the fan bypass.

The P3.0 pressure tap allows the combustor inlet pressure to be read by the main fuel control. The {T 5.4} station is the location for the Inter Turbine Temperature harness, which reads the exhaust gas temperature between the HPT and the LPT. This signal goes to the flight deck for readout on EICAS.
