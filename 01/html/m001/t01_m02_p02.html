Functional Description

Each frame consist of an inner and outer casing that are supported by the struts.

The front frame contains the A-sump. Within the A-sump, bearings No. 1, 2 and 3 support the fan (N1) and compressor rotor (N2). The A-sump is sealed at the front by carbon seal No.1 and at the rear by carbon seal No. 3.

The combustion chamber frame contains the B-sump. Within the B-sump, bearings No. 4 and 5 support the N2 rotor. The B-sump is sealed at the front by No. 4 carbon seal, and at the aft by No. 5 carbon seal.

The exhaust frame contains the C-sump. Within the C-sump, bearings No. 6 and No. 7 support the N1 rotor. The C-sump is sealed at the front by carbon seal No. 6 and at the aft by No. 7 carbon seal.
