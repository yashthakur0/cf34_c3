Purpose

The combustion chamber frame is the second of three major structural frames in the engine. The frame houses the B-sump and therefore supports the No. 4 and 5 roller bearings. Externally, the frame has a series of bosses to allow for installation of the fuel injectors, ignitors and a P3.0 pressure port. The frame also has a port for extraction of 14 stage bleed air for operation of the thrust reverser and for aircraft anti-ice systems.
