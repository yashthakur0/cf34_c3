Functional Description

The combustion chamber frame provides the structural support to the B-sump. The struts are used to route the following servicing lines and sensors into and out of the sump:

  • 7th stage unregulated seal air pressure
  • Oil scavenge
  • B-sump vent
  • B-sump aft scavenge
  • B-sump forward scavenge, B-sump oil supply
