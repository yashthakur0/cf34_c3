Functional Description

The exhaust frame provides the structural support to the C-sump. The struts are used to route the following servicing lines into and out of the sump:

  • Regulated seal pressurization air
  • C-sump forward scavenge
  • C-sump aft scavenge
  • C-sump oil supply
  • C-sump seal drain
