Identification

The pre-modification B-sump vent regulator valve is mounted vertically at the 3 o'clock position on the compressor case. The valve has four tube connections. The connection on the upper left side of the valve housing is connected to the compressor seventh stage bleed port. The connection on the lower right side connects to the A-sump through an air tube into the 12 o'clock strut. The bottom connection on the valve housing is connected to the B-sump through the B-sump scavenge and vent tube. The top connection supplies a reference {PT 5.4} pressure at the transition assembly. A piston and spring is installed into the valve housing.
