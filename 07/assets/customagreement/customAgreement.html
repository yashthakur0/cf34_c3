Course Completion
  • Please read through all the content provided and complete all the interactions in the course.
  • You need to review minimum 75% of the content in order to complete the course.
  • Also, a score of at least 75% is required to pass the post assessments that appears at the end of modules.
Exiting the Course
  • To exit the course at any time, use the Power icon/button. This will ensure that your progress is tracked and saved.
  • Do not exit using the Close [X] button. It will not save your score or track your course status.
