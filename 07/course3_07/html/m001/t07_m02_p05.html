Operation

Based on speed and temperature inputs supplied to the Main Fuel Control, a fuel pressure is applied to both VG actuators simultaneously from the fuel control, moving the actuator shafts and attached actuator rings.

The actuating rings are attached to each variable vane arm on the IGV and stages 1-5. The rings rotate, moving all vanes in each stage simultaneously. The vanes are in a closed position during start and low power, and rotate to an open, or an in-line position, as power is increased.

The mechanical feedback cable connects the 2 o'clock actuating shaft to the Main Fuel Control cable arm. This is a position feedback, requiring adjustment for proper system operation.
