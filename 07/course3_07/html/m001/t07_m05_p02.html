Compressor discharge air:

  • Also used to surround the B-sump housing.
    • Helps to isolate it from high combustor flow path temperatures.
  • Then flows aft under the stage 1 and 2 turbine disks into the LPT area and into the gas path at various locations.
  • In the gas path ,where a rotating and stationary component interface, air is used as the sealing medium.
