Identification

The B-sump vent regulator valve is mounted vertically at the 3 o'clock position on the compressor case. The valve has three tube connections. The connection on the upper left side of the valve housing is connected to the compressor seventh stage bleed port. The connection on the lower right side connects to the A-sump through an air tube into the 12 o'clock strut. The bottom connection on the valve housing is connected to the B-sump through the B-sump scavenge and vent tube. Circular orifice plates are installed into the seventh stage and A-sump fittings on the valve housing.
