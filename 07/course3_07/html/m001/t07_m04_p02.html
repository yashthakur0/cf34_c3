Functional description

The parasitic air system draws air from the 7th, 10th and 14 stage of compression for the following purposes:

  • Stage 7
    • The high pressure from the engine is regulated to a lower pressure by the carbon seal pressure regulating valve
    • Regulated air pressure is routed to:
    • No. 1 and No. 3 carbon seals in the A-sump
    • No. 6 carbon seal in the C-sump.
    • Unregulated air pressure is routed to the B-sump for pressurization of No. 4 and No. 5 carbon seals.
  • Stage 10
    • Used to supply air and pressure to the cabin, through the Environmental Control System.
    • Used as a cross start source of air pressure for the opposite engine.
  • Stage 14
    • Used as a pressure source for:
    • Operation of the thrust reverser system
    • Engine nacelle anti-ice
    • High air pressure and high temperature source
