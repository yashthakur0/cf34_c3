Identification

The distribution and control subsystems:

  • Located on the engine, in the airframe and in the cockpit.
  • Consist of the following components:
    • Start/ignition control panel
    • Speed cutout switch
    • Air starter valve
    • Air turbine starter (ATS)
  • Similar, but independent pneumatic ducting and electrical wiring on each engine connect the subsystems on the engine with the subsystems on the aircraft.
