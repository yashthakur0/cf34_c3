Purpose

The distribution and control subsystems have the following purpose:

  • Initiate the starting process from the cockpit
  • Supply electrical current to the air starter valve to energize it
  • Supply air to the ATS at a pressure that is sufficient to turn it
  • Control the buildup of air pressure within the air starter valve
  • Transmit rotary motion of the ATS to the AGB
  • Make sure that during the start cycle, the engine can be stopped in an emergency
  • Make sure that after starting, when N2 becomes approximately 55%, the air starter valve is automatically de-energized
