Operation

  • When the START switch is pressed and held for two seconds, a DC current of 28 volts flows from the battery to the speed cutout switch and then to the start relay.
  • Current flows to the air starter valve solenoid, energizing it and opening the air starter valve.
  • When N2 reaches a value of approx 55%, an electrical signal from the monopole speed sensor in the ATS opens the speed cutout switch contact points.
  • This terminates the flow of electric current from the speed cutout switch to the start relay, stopping the electrical power to the air starter valve solenoid.
    • The air starter valve closes.
