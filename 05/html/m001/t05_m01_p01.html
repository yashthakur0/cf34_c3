Purpose

The starting system:

  • Designed to start the engine by using mechanical power to rotate the compressor rotor through the radial drive shaft and the power takeoff assembly.
  • The mechanical power is obtained by driving the ATS with high pressure air from any of the following sources:
    • APU
    • Cross feed from an operating engine
    • External ground source
  • The starting system makes sure that the engine can be started under following conditions:
    • In stationary condition - when on ground
    • In windmilling condition - when airborne
  • For airborne starts, the minimum allowable N2 for starting with or without the ATS is:
    • At altitudes below 10,000 feet: approximately 10% of N2
    • At altitudes above 10,000 feet: approximately 13% of N2
