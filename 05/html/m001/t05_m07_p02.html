Operation

  • When the "A" ignition switch or the "B" ignition switch is pressed
    • The ARM ON legend on that switch is illuminated, indicating that the ignition system is armed and ready for operation.
    • If the CONT switch is pressed, then the ARM ON legends on both "A" and "B" switches are illuminated.
  • When the START switch for left or right-hand engine is pressed and held for two seconds
    • The contacts in the switch close and the START legend on the switch is illuminated, indicating that the start cycle on the engine has been initiated.
    • After the engine attains approx 55% N2, the START legend light turns off.
