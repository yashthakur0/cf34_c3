Identification

The air turbine starter (ATS)

  • Installed on a drive pad on the rear face of the AGB at 4 oâ€™clock position.
  • Lightweight, single-stage, axial-flow turbine.
  • Has the following components:
    • Inlet assembly
    • Reduction gear assembly
    • Engagement mechanism
    • Lubrication system
    • Splined output shaft assembly
    • Monopole speed sensor
  • The inlet assembly has a single-stage stator, an axial-flow turbine wheel and a containment shield.
  • The reduction gear assembly has a carrier assembly, planetary gears and a gear shaft.
  • The engagement mechanism has a sprag clutch and a clutch carrier.
  • The lubrication system consists of a gerotor-type pump, oil filler plug and a magnetic drain and chip detector.
  • The splined output shaft assembly has a splined output shaft with a shear section.
    • An emergency decoupler is also mounted in the shaft assembly.
