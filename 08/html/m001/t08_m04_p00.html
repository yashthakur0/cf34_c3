Identification

The fuel filter:

  • Mounted at approx 11 oâ€™clock position on the compressor casing, just aft of the lubricating oil tank.
  • Primarily consists of the following components:
    • Filter housing
    • Filter bowl
    • Filter element
  • The filter housing has an inlet port, an outlet port and a bypass valve.
    • A red warning button on the bypass valve protrudes outside the housing.
    • The top of the housing has a fuel temperature sensing port and an electrical connector for the impending bypass switch.
  • The filter bowl is screwed to the threads in the housing.
    • The bowl has a drain port at the bottom.
  • The filter element is a disposable corrugated paper unit and is installed inside the filter bowl.
    • The filter element has a nominal filtration rating of 10 microns.
