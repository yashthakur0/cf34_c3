Identification

The fuel indicating subsystem:

  • Comprises of the process and components by which information about fuel flow, fuel temperature and fuel pressure is provided for visual display inside and outside the cockpit.
  • Includes the following components:
    • Airframe manufacturer-supplied fuel flowmeter between the MFC and the fuel manifold.
    • Airframe manufacturer-supplied fuel low pressure switch connected to the inlet pressure tap on the fuel pump.
    • Fuel temperature sensing port and airframe manufacturer-supplied thermocouple on the fuel filter housing.
    • Impending bypass switch and electrical connector on the fuel filter housing.
    • Bypass indication button on the fuel filter housing.
  • The red-colored bypass indication button provides visual display on the fuel filter housing.
  • The rest of the components transmit fuel information to the cockpit and display it on the Primary page and the Secondary page of EICAS (Engine Indication and Crew Alerting System).
