Identification

The fuel manifold:

  • Consists of two separate, semi circular halves of tubing that are attached together with B-nuts at 12 o’clock and 6 o’clock positions to form one continuous ring.
  • Circles the outside of the combustion chamber frame and is supported by several brackets.
  • Eighteen fuel injector hoses form part of the manifold.
