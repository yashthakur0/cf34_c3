Purpose

In the fuel distribution subsystem, the MFC serves the following purpose:

    • Receive pressurized discharge fuel from the primary stage of the fuel pump.
    • Supply metered fuel through the fuel flowmeter to the fuel manifold and the fuel injectors.
    • Supply pressurized fuel to the left and righthand VG actuators.
    • Return bypass fuel back to the primary stage of the fuel pump.
    • Supply fuel to the T2C sensor.
