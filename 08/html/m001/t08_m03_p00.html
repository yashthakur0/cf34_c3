Identification

The fuel pump:

  • Mounted on the aft side of the AGB.
    • Splined to the upper left AGB drive pad and is held in place with a V-band clamp.
  • Self-lubricating and consists of the following:
    • One low pressure boost impeller stage
    • One high pressure primary gear stage
    • One high pressure secondary gear stage
    • Two relief valves
    • One drive shaft that is splined to the AGB.
    • One main fuel control coupling shaft that is splined to the MFC
  • Has the following ports:
    • Inlet for fuel from the aircraft ejector boost pumps.
    • Inlet for fuel from the fuel filter.
    • Inlet for fuel into the secondary stage.
    • Inlet for bypass return fuel from the MFC.
    • Outlet for boost stage discharge fuel to the heat exchanger and to the secondary stage inlet.
    • Outlet for primary stage discharge fuel to the MFC.
    • Outlet for secondary stage discharge as motive flow to the aircraft fuel tank.
  • Several pressure tapping points are provided to allow inputs to the cockpit indicating devices.
