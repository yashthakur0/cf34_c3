Purpose

The fuel pump serves the following purpose:

    • It boosts the inlet pressure of the fuel received from the aircraft ejector boost pumps, making sure that the fuel has sufficient pressure to flow through the heat exchanger and the fuel filter.
    • It provides pressurized fuel to the MFC.
    • It provides the N2 input signal to the MFC.
    • It provides motive flow for the operation of the aircraft ejector boost pumps.
