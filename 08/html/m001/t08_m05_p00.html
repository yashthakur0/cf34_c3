Identification

The main fuel control (MFC):

  • Clamped to the aft flange of the fuel pump with a V-band clamp.
    • A coupling shaft physically connects the MFC to the fuel pump.
  • Internally consists of a number of rotating and stationary components.
  • Has a total of seven ports for fuel distribution.
    • Two of the ports are located on the flange end.
    • One port is located on the flange surface.
    • The other port is located on the circumference around the recess for the coupling shaft.
    • When mounting the MFC with the fuel pump, both these ports mate with the corresponding ports on the fuel pump.
    • Two of the ports are on the aft end and are connected to the left and right-hand VG actuators through a system of tubing.
    • One of the ports is located on the aft end and connects the MFC to the airframe manufacturer-supplied fuel flowmeter.
    • Two of the ports are located on the aft end and are connected to the T2C sensor through a system of tubing.
